<objective>
Fix the GlitchTip `migrate` and `seed` Jobs that are blocked by the error-tracking
NetworkPolicies. Add a `template.metadata.labels` block (carrying
`app.kubernetes.io/name: glitchtip`) to both Job pod templates so their pods match
the additive-allow NetworkPolicies and can reach `glitchtip-postgres:5432`.

Purpose: Pods inherit labels ONLY from `template.metadata.labels`; the Job-object
`metadata.labels` do NOT propagate to pods. Under the Phase 17 default-deny egress/
ingress regime, the label-less migrate/seed pods are denied egress to postgres
(`allow-glitchtip-db-egress` selects `app.kubernetes.io/name=glitchtip`) and denied
by postgres ingress (`allow-glitchtip-postgres-ingress` accepts only the same label),
so `pg_isready` / `showmigrations` never succeed and the Jobs time out. The web/worker
Deployments in 91-glitchtip.yaml already set this template-label block, which is why
they pass the netpols — this brings the Jobs to parity.

Output: Two edited manifests, each with the load-bearing pod-template label block.
The fix lives on the pods (least-privilege preserved); the NetworkPolicies are NOT
touched.
</objective>
