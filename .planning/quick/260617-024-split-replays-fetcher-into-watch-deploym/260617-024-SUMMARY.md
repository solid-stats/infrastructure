# Phase quick Plan 260617-024: Split replays-fetcher into watch Deployment + nightly run-once CronJob Summary

Split the single `replays-fetcher` CronJob into a continuous page-1 watch Deployment plus a nightly full-corpus run-once CronJob, both pinned to image `9f091bf...`, sharing one ServiceAccount; validated offline with `kubectl apply --dry-run=client`.

## What Was Built

- **k8s/staging/50-replays-fetcher.yaml** (repurposed): ServiceAccount `replays-fetcher` left unchanged as the single shared SA. The CronJob became the nightly full-corpus sweep: `schedule: "0 8 * * *"`, `timeZone: Europe/Moscow`, `suspend: false` (was `true`/`*/30`), `args: ["run-once"]`, image bumped to the pinned `9f091bf...`. Preserved `backoffLimit: 30` (with its F4 comment), `concurrencyPolicy: Forbid`, full resilience env block (including `S3_CHECKPOINT_CONDITIONAL_WRITES`), `envFrom replays-fetcher-runtime`, resources (100m/256Mi req, 1/1Gi limit), priorityClassName, securityContext, the REPLAY_SOURCE_MAX_PAGES "intentionally unset" block, and history limits.
- **k8s/staging/52-replays-fetcher-watch.yaml** (new): `kind: Deployment` `replays-fetcher-watch`, `replicas: 1`, `args: ["watch"]`, same pinned image. Source/S3 env only (S3_ENDPOINT, S3_REGION, S3_FORCE_PATH_STYLE, RETRY_ATTEMPTS, REQUEST_SPACING_MS, CONCURRENCY, TIMEOUT_MS); the S3 checkpoint conditional-writes knob and REPLAY_WATCH_INTERVAL_MS are intentionally absent. `envFrom replays-fetcher-runtime`; `serviceAccountName: replays-fetcher` (shared); `automountServiceAccountToken: false`; `priorityClassName: app-critical`; `imagePullSecrets: ghcr-pull`; `imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent`; `terminationGracePeriodSeconds: 30`; Burstable resources (100m/256Mi req, 1/1Gi limit); securityContext allowPrivilegeEscalation:false + drop ALL. Liveness probe is an exec `node -e` heartbeat-freshness check (`<60s` against `/tmp/replays-fetcher-watch.heartbeat`, env-fallback honored), `initialDelaySeconds: 30`, `periodSeconds: 20`, `failureThreshold: 3`. No readiness probe, no HTTP. Explicit namespace `solid-stats-staging` and standard app labels.
