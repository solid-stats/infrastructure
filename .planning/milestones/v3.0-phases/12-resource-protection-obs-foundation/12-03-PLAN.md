<objective>
Patch all six runtime workload manifests to carry `priorityClassName: app-critical` (PREP-03 assignment) and bring postgres + server-2 to Guaranteed QoS by equalising requests==limits on every container, including server-2's two busybox initContainers (PREP-04). QoS numbers are authored as the documented ASSUMED conservative values and flagged for live confirmation in Plan 05.

Purpose: PriorityClass separation is the primary node-pressure eviction protection; Guaranteed QoS on the two protected workloads is the secondary layer (kernel OOM + last-in-QoS-ranking). This plan only edits manifest files — it does NOT apply them to the live cluster (that, plus the rolling restart it triggers and the live `kubectl top` confirmation of QoS values, is Plan 05). Manifests stay validate-staging.py-clean (serviceAccountName, automountServiceAccountToken:false, resources/requests/limits, securityContext all preserved). Autonomous; parallel with Plans 01 and 02. The QoS change touching postgres/server-2 forces a pod restart on apply — that live impact is gated in Plan 05.
Output: edits to the six k8s/staging/*.yaml workload manifests.
</objective>
